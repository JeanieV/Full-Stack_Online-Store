Our Story

The Art of Pottery is a South African based company that works directly with our local partners from Cape Town to create sustainable, functional and contemporary objects for your home.

All of our products are designed and hand crafted by us here in South Africa.

We take great pride in preserving traditional methods of production, in many cases using practices that have been passed down generations but often overlooked.

We believe handcrafted products have an inherent individuality, with unique differences revealing how they have been made, setting them apart from mass produced items.

Meet the team

Our team consists of 5 lovely ladies from Cape Town, who are deeply passionate about the art of pottery.

We're not just a pottery store; we're a close-knit family of artisans who have come together to share our love for crafting beautiful ceramics with the world.

We partner with local artists from Cape Town to empower them so that the world can share in their talent.
